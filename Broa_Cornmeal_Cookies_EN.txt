Ingredients:
1 cup of Cornmeal
1 cup of flour
1 teaspoon of Baking powder
1/2 cup of Sugar 
3 tablespoons of Butter
1 Egg yolk (for the egg wash)
Fennel seeds (to taste)

A little bit of milk (drops if mixture is too dry)

Preparation Method:

Mix the drys (Cornmeal, Flour, Baking Powder, Sugar) 
add the butter
mix until sandy texture
add a little bit of milk until the dough becomes homogenous (not too much)
add the fennel seeds

In a parchment paper lined baking tray, put meatball sized doughballs (with about an inch of space between them) , flatten the dough balls and brush the egg wash.

Put it in a 180C (fan) oven for 20-25 minutes until golden brown delicious. like a tan colour not too brown nor not too light.
